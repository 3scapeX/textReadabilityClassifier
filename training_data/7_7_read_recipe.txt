A recipe from India
Chicken curry

You need:
300 g chicken breast
150 g yoghurt
1 tablespoon curry paste
salt, pepper
2 onions, 1 red chilli
oil

1. Cut 300 g chicken breast into pieces.
2. Mix 150 g yoghurt and 1 tablespoon curry paste.
3. Put the chicken pieces into a flat dish. Add some salt and pepper. Then cover them with the yoghurt mixture.
4. Cut two onions and one red chilli into thin slices and fry them in oil.
5. Add the chicken to the pan and cook it slowly for 40 minutes.
6. Serve with rice.
