Visiting the Science Museum

Planning a visit – Science Museum

Opening times and admission costs
The Science Museum is open every day from 10 am to 6 pm. Entrance is free but some activities require money.
Arrive early!
If you have booked a special event or the cinema, please arrive at the museum at least 30 minutes before your scheduled time.
Eating areas
School groups can eat in the picnic area in the basement, on the benches in the museum, or in quiet areas. You can buy snacks from the Eat Drink Shop in the basement. School groups are not allowed to use the museum’s cafés.
Coats and bags
At the entrance we provide lockers for coats and bags. Space is limited, so please bring as little as possible.
