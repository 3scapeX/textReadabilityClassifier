At the ticket counter

Rajiv: Two tickets to Cardiff, please.
Woman: Single or return?
Rajiv: Return, please.
Woman: OK, that’s £ 108 pounds altogether.
Rajiv: Here you are. Oh, what platform is it, please?
Woman: Platform 8.
Rajiv: Thank you. And could you tell us when the next train leaves?
Woman: You have to hurry. It leaves in five minutes.
