Texting rules

Always be polite when you’re texting.
Don’t send a text message when you’re angry.
If you receive horrible text messages, show them to your parents or your teachers. 
Help other kids who receive bullying text messages.
Don’t text during meals, during lessons or when you’re talking to someone.
Don’t send text messages when you’re riding your bike or walking down the street.
Turn off your mobile phone at night.
